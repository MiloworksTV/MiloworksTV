SHOW: "Milo" -- 2D cartoon short series. Pocoyo simplicity +
Adventure Time heart + Hilda warmth. Hand-drawn-feel cartoon
craft, NOT psychedelic. Looks like a modern studio's production.

THE SHOW IS SILENT. No spoken dialogue ever. No voiceover. No
narration. Characters never open their mouths to speak. Story is
told through morphs, eye expressions, antenna movements, gestures,
deadpan reactions, thought-bubble emoji that pop above heads, and
diegetic on-screen text on signs / sticky notes / magazine pages.

VISUAL: Clean 2D traditional animation. Thick uniform black
outlines, flat cel-shaded fills with two-tone shadows. Vibrant
neon-pastel palette (mint greens, hot pinks, warm oranges,
lavender skies). Squash-and-stretch on Milo only; the world stays
solid -- grass is grass, houses are houses. NO melting backgrounds,
NO rainbow shifts, NO character auras, NO pulsing bodies. Sparkle
FX extremely sparingly. Static locked camera by default.
Plano-secuencia: every shot picks up where the previous left off.

WHO APPEARS: Characters appear ONLY when the SHOT below names
them. If the shot does not name MILO or PIP, they must not appear.

MILO -- Small mint-green teardrop alien blob, knee-high. Two
wobbly antennae with round tips. ONE large round expressive eye.
NO MOUTH, ever. Soft mint glow. Thick black outline. Glides on
his base, no legs. Morphs expressively (hearts, question marks,
stretches, splits) and always returns to default teardrop.

PIP -- 9-year-old human girl. Messy chocolate-brown ponytail.
Horizontally striped pastel t-shirt (mint, cream, coral). Denim
shorts. Bare feet. Deadpan half-lidded brown eyes. MOUTH ALWAYS
CLOSED in a flat line (or sealed on her juice-box straw). She
never speaks. Juice box with bendy straw in her hand. Thick black
outline.

THOUGHT-BUBBLE LANGUAGE: A small mint or white cartoon
thought-bubble can pop above a character's head containing one of:
'!', '?', red heart, lightbulb, green check, red X, sleeping Z.
Pops in with a soft cartoon 'pop', holds, fades. The show's main
'dialogue' replacement.

WORLD: 1990s American suburban backyard at golden hour or twilight
-- green lawn, white picket fence, blue plastic kiddie pool at the
left edge, ceramic garden gnome with red hat, striped folding lawn
chair on the patio, beige house with sliding glass door,
peach-to-lavender pastel sky.

AUDIO RULE: Every shot's audio is ambient + non-verbal SFX only.
Birds, sprinklers, soft breeze, distant dog, cartoon 'pops' for
bubbles, 'boings' for stretches. NEVER include voices, dialogue,
narration, or voiceover. Every audio block ends with the literal
'NO VOICES. NO DIALOGUE.' Music is post-mixed at stitch time,
never described in the prompt.

FORMAT: 16:9 widescreen, 2D flat cartoon, no live action / 3D /
CGI. TONE: bright, warm, sweet, gently sarcastic via Pip's
deadpan stares, never mean, never scary.
